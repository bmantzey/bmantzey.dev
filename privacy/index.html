Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Information We Collect

SwiftFolio respects your privacy. We collect the following information:

  • Email address - Used for account creation and sign-in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Optional display name and company/about text - For guest log entries if you choose to provide them.
  • Approximate location - Only if you sign the guest log, to display your pin on the map.
  • Anonymous user ID - Generated automatically by Firebase for internal account management.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect:

  • Physical address or contact information beyond email
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising data or identifiers
  • Precise GPS coordinates (only city/region level location)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Authenticating your account
  • Displaying your guest log entry on the map (if you choose to sign)
  • Showing your resume within the app

Data Sharing

Your information is not shared with third parties except as necessary for app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google) - For authentication and database services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or marketing companies.

Data Security

Your data is secured using:

  • Firebase Authentication with industry-standard encryption
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Firebase Security Rules to protect your data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request a copy of your information

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app, all your data (email, guest log entries) will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Children's Privacy

SwiftFolio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
