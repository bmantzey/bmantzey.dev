Frequently Asked Questions

What is SwiftFolio?
SwiftFolio is a professional portfolio app that showcases iOS development skills and experience. It includes Firebase authentication, an interactive guest log with a global map, and a full resume viewer.
How do I sign the guest log?
First, create an account or sign in using the authentication screen. Once signed in, navigate to the Guest Log tab and tap "Go To My Entry" to add your message and location to the map.
Is my information private?
Yes. Your authentication information is securely stored in Firebase. Guest log entries show only the information you choose to share (name, company, and message). Email addresses are never publicly displayed.
Can I view the source code?
Yes! SwiftFolio is open source. You can view the complete source code on GitHub. The link is available both in the app and on this support page.
What technologies are used?
SwiftFolio is built with SwiftUI, Firebase Authentication (developed using Test-Driven Development), MapKit for the interactive map, and PDFKit for the resume viewer.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email me at bmantzey@mac.com with details about the issue, including your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the problem. You can also open an issue on the GitHub repository.
Are you available for iOS development work?
Yes! I'm a Senior iOS Engineer with 16+ years of experience. Feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn to discuss opportunities.

Privacy

SwiftFolio respects your privacy. We collect minimal information:

  • Email and password for authentication (stored securely in Firebase)
  • Guest log entries you choose to create (name, company, message, approximate location)
